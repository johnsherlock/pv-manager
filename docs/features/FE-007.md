# FE-007: Auth and Onboarding Foundation

## Goal

Turn the rewrite from a seeded local-user app into a real account-based product
that can admit invited beta users, authenticate them safely, and guide them
through the first-run setup needed to connect their installation.

This feature establishes the minimum account and onboarding foundation for beta:

- approval-gated sign-up and sign-in
- terms/privacy acceptance as part of account creation
- first-run onboarding and setup flow
- secure capture of MyEnergi credentials per installation

## Status

`Todo`

## Feature Completion Criteria

- Approved users can sign in and reach the rewrite app without seed-only hacks
- Auth requirements, invite gating, and terms/privacy acceptance are defined
- First-run onboarding exists with a coherent path from account creation to
  connected installation
- MyEnergi credentials are captured server-side and linked to the persisted
  provider connection rather than deployment-level env vars
- Auth and onboarding edge cases are identified clearly enough for later
  implementation and verification

## Deferred From This Feature

- Gifted pro access and premium entitlement management
- Broader admin approval tooling beyond what auth/onboarding needs
- Public marketing, welcome, and demo pages beyond the minimal beta-entry path

## Notes

- This feature is intentionally about making the app usable by real beta users,
  not about broad self-serve growth or premium packaging.
- Approval-gated access-request UX is grouped separately under the public and
  unauthenticated experience feature.
