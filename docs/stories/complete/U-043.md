# U-043: Financial charts — cost histogram and period breakdown

## Objective

Add the two financial charts to the Range History page: a per-day cost histogram
that shows actual cost vs the without-solar baseline, and a period cost breakdown
chart that shows how the total energy cost was composed.

## Scope

### Chart 1: Per-day cost histogram
- Per-day bars: actual net cost (€) and without-solar net cost (€)
- The difference (solar savings) should be visually clear — either as a separate
  bar or as an overlay/delta annotation
- Export credit shown as a distinct segment or separate bar series
- Tooltip: date, actual net cost, without-solar cost, savings, export credit
- Days with no tariff coverage render with a visual indicator (no financial bar)

### Chart 2: Period cost breakdown (donut)
Shows the composition of the total equivalent energy cost for the period:
- Import cost (€) — cost of grid import at applicable rates
- Fixed charges (€) — standing charges and other fixed costs
- Export credit (€) — value earned from exported energy (shown as a credit
  reducing total cost)
- Solar savings (€) — cost avoided by solar self-consumption

The intent: "for the total energy you used, here is how the cost broke down and
what solar contributed."

**Rate-band note:** import cost uses banded rates (day/peak/night) from P-039
when available. When the API signals `note: 'simplified-daily-rate'`, add a
visible label on the chart: "Import costs estimated at day rate (time-of-use data
not available for this period)."

**Free import slot:** reserve a visual segment in the donut for free import kWh
with a "not active" placeholder — this makes it straightforward to activate later
without redesigning the chart.

### Shared behaviour
- Both charts re-render when the period selector changes
- ECharts canvas-rendered
- In the no-tariff state, both charts are replaced by a prompt card ("Add a tariff
  to see financial breakdowns")

## Out of Scope

- Solar coverage and ratio charts (U-044)
- Rate-band breakdown in the no-solar baseline (known simplification, documented
  in P-039)
- Free import classification (deferred)

## Acceptance Criteria

- Per-day cost histogram renders actual vs without-solar cost per day
- Solar savings are visually distinguishable from the actual cost
- Period breakdown donut shows import cost, fixed charges, export credit, and
  solar savings
- Banded billing is reflected when `note` is `'banded-daily-rate'`; simplified
  label shown when `note` is `'simplified-daily-rate'`
- No-tariff state replaces charts with a prompt card
- Both charts re-render correctly when the period changes

## Dependencies

- `U-041` (page shell)
- `P-039` (banded billing in range API — needed for accurate cost charts)
