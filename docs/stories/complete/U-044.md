# U-044: Solar coverage, export ratio, and payback tracker

## Objective

Add the remaining analytical modules to the Range History page: the solar
coverage area chart, the export ratio chart, and (for financed installations)
the payback progress tracker.

## Scope

### Chart 1: Solar coverage (green energy %) — area chart
- Daily percentage of total consumption met by solar generation
- Source: `selfConsumptionRatio` field from daily summary series
- Area chart with a soft fill (consistent with v1 green energy % chart)
- X-axis: local date; Y-axis: percentage (0–100%)
- Tooltip: date + solar coverage %
- Data gaps show as breaks, not interpolated

### Chart 2: Export ratio — area or bar chart
New in v2 — not in v1.
- Daily percentage of total generated energy that was exported to the grid
  (vs self-consumed or diverted to immersion)
- Formula: `exportKwh / generatedKwh` per day (null/zero when no generation)
- Helps users understand how well-matched their consumption pattern is to their
  generation profile — a high export ratio on summer days means most generation
  is going to the grid rather than being used on-site
- Tooltip: date + export ratio %

### Payback progress bar
Only rendered when `installation.financeMode` is not null.
- Cumulative solar savings to date (from period start) vs cumulative finance
  payments over the same period
- Display: a horizontal progress-style bar showing savings vs payment for the
  selected period, with a note of the total finance term and monthly payment
- Source: `summary.solar.savings` (period savings) from range API;
  `monthlyFinancePaymentAmount` and `financeTermMonths` from installation record
- If the selected period's savings exceed the period's finance payments, style
  positively; if not, show neutrally with context ("solar saved €X of your €Y
  in payments this period")
- Do not attempt to project remaining payback time — show only the selected
  period's relationship between savings and payments

### Shared behaviour
- All modules re-render when the period selector changes
- ECharts for both charts
- Export ratio chart hidden (not just empty) when no generation data exists for
  the period

## Out of Scope

- Rolling payback projection or break-even date estimate (deferred)
- Self-consumption ratio trend as a separate chart — already covered by solar
  coverage (they measure the same thing from consumption's perspective)

## Acceptance Criteria

- Solar coverage area chart renders per-day % with data gap breaks
- Export ratio chart renders per-day % and is hidden when generation is absent
- Payback progress bar renders only for financed installations
- Payback bar correctly reflects the period's savings vs equivalent finance
  payments
- All modules re-render correctly when the period changes
