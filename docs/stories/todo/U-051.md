# U-051: Define investment recovery and payoff insight surfaces

## Objective

Define the first downstream product surfaces unlocked by Finance setup so the
app can explain how much of the user's solar investment has been recovered,
whether selected periods cover repayments, and when the system may be paid off,
while staying honest about incomplete history and prediction limits.

## Scope

- Define the first finance insight modules for signed-in users
- Decide where each insight belongs in the product, including:
  - Overview-style setup/progress context
  - Range History period interpretation
  - lifetime-style recovery view when enough history exists
- Define the first insight set:
  - amount recovered from available recorded history
  - estimated total recovered since investment date when history is incomplete
  - selected-period savings vs repayment coverage
  - simple payoff estimate based on historical savings behavior
- Define trust and wording rules when:
  - history starts after the investment date
  - tariff coverage is incomplete
  - provider coverage is incomplete
  - finance setup is missing
- Define how repayment-coverage messaging behaves for:
  - upfront-only users
  - repayment users
  - mixed upfront-plus-repayment users
- Define copy that makes projections feel like guidance, not promises

## Out of Scope

- Complex forecasting models
- Multi-investment-event attribution
- Tax/accounting reporting
- Dedicated Data Health feature design beyond the finance-specific trust wording

## Acceptance Criteria

- The first finance insights are documented with clear screen ownership
- Incomplete-history language distinguishes recorded recovery vs estimated total recovery
- Selected-period repayment coverage rules are defined for month/year/range views
- Payoff estimate rules are simple, explicit, and caveated appropriately
- Missing-finance and partial-history states have intentional UX treatment
